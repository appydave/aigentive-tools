Post Hook Strategy

Use one of the following types of engaging hooks to help increase attention and interaction across your content this month.

1. Question Hook

"Struggling with chronic back pain? Swipe to discover the top 3 exercises to alleviate it!"

2. Surprising Fact Hook

"Did you know that sitting for more than 8 hours a day can reduce your lifespan? Swipe to learn how to combat this with simple stretches!"

3. Bold Statement Hook

"Fix your posture in just one week! Swipe to see the exercises that will transform your spine."

4. Challenge Hook

"Think you're flexible? Swipe to test your flexibility with these 5 stretches!"
